Stephanie Marcellé

Bio

Stephanie Marcellé is a beautiful singer/songwriter who acoustically blends pop, soulful hip hop, and country-tinged folk from the heart of New Orleans. An accomplished pianist, she often uses the black and white keys to compliment her passionate voice, rhythmic twists and turns, and raw, emotive lyrics, crafting original songs and covers that are truly one of a kinds.
